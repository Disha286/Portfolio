About me

Code with
a designer's
instinct

"I don't just build things — I build things that feel right."

I'm a CS student who refused to pick a lane. I care deeply about how software looks, how it performs, and what it actually does for the people using it. That's why I've shipped a live SaaS product, trained a computer vision model, and led a product design engagement for a real industry client — and I'm just getting started.

I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of design and engineering — where a thoughtful UI decision saves a developer three months of re-work, or where the right data visualisation makes a stakeholder's decision obvious. When I'm not coding I'm in Figma, and when I'm in Figma I'm thinking about the API. That overlap is where I do my best work.
